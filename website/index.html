Enjoy a quick meal using your MKT tokens!

NEO Food is a decentralized marketplace for restaurants or small businesses to connect with their clients and deliver them their meal, with a simple, easy and fast payment process. It uses smart contracts to ensure satisfaction from both sides!

  • Click on the desired meal below
  • Scan the QR code with your NEON Wallet
  • Fill in the order details
  • Make sure to scan the QR code upon the delivery!
